## Decision 002 - Multimodal Embedding Strategy
**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Team Members Present:** Tech, Research, DA

**Decision:** Separate encoders + early fusion (concatenation) with fallback to cross-modal attention

**Options Considered:**
1. CLIP (vision-text only)
2. ImageBind (6 modalities, large model)
3. Separate encoders + early fusion
4. Cross-modal attention
5. Multimodal transformer (LMM-based)

**Rationale:**
- Option 1 (CLIP): Too limited (no audio/IMU)
- Option 2 (ImageBind): Too large (~3GB) for on-device
- Option 3 (early fusion): Feasible, flexible, optimized per modality
- Option 4 (cross-modal): Too complex for timeline
- Option 5 (LMM): Training required, timeline too tight

**Trade-offs:**
- Simplicity: Early fusion is simpler (✅)
- Performance: May be suboptimal vs learned joint representations (❌)
- Timeline: Fits 2-week implementation window (✅)

**Fallback Plan:**
- If evaluation shows poor performance (especially on cross-modal tasks)
- Upgrade to cross-modal attention (adds 1-2 weeks)
- Document as "initial approach" in thesis

**Owner:** Technical Lead
**Deadline:** Week 8 (implementation complete)
**Confidence:** Medium-High (based on Research's literature review)

---
